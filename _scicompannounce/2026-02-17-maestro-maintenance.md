---
Title: maestro maintenance on February 24
---

As part of the [E2 Data Center Work](/_scicompannounce/2025-12-07-e2-data-center-work) scheduled for February 24, 2026, `maestro` will be temporarily offline.
This is the final week of the E2 Data Center Work.

- On February 24, 2026 we will shutdown `maestro` which will close any running processes, connections, tmux/screen sessions etc you may have running.
- It will remain offline for several hours.
- Only `maestro` will be impacted by this maintenance.
