## Open Specimen For Specimen Data Management

[OpenSpecimen](https://www.openspecimen.org/) is a highly configurable web-based Biobanking Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) used in 70+ clinical research centers across 20+ countries - including many of the worldâ€™s leading research centers. OpenSpecimen helps you collect High-quality Biospecimen Data and track biospecimens from Collection to Utilization. 

[The Office of Translational Research](https://centernet.fredhutch.org/u/otr.html) (OTR) provides OpenSpecimen at Fred Hutch. Their [CenterNet page](https://centernet.fredhutch.org/u/otr/specimen-management-tool.html) contains more information about features and how to submit an intake request.
